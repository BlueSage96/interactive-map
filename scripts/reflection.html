My predictions/Reflection

Overall, I am a bit disheartended about these cases. I am glad that many people are finally to push back even more with protests. However, I do not condone looting and rioting nor attacking police officers who are trying to help. I try to do my part by sharing these stories on social media. I have not joined a protest mainly because the one in my hometown was not advertised well. As for the future, I believe that there will be more positive changes than set backs when it comes to the question if black lives matter.
